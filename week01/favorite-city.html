GET TO KNOW PRAGUE

Prague is the capital of the Czech Republic and one of the most beautiful cities in Europe. Known for its medieval architecture, cobblestone streets, and rich history, Prague attracts millions of visitors every year. The city is divided by the Vltava River and is famous for landmarks such as Charles Bridge, Prague Castle, and the historic Old Town Square. Its mix of Gothic, Baroque, and Renaissance buildings creates a unique atmosphere that feels both historic and lively.

Beyond its architecture, Prague is also known for its culture, food, and vibrant social life. The city offers a wide variety of cafés, traditional Czech restaurants, museums, and music events. Visitors can enjoy local dishes such as goulash and trdelník while exploring neighborhoods full of art and nightlife. Prague is also considered a student-friendly and affordable destination compared to many other European capitals, making it popular among international travelers, digital nomads, and exchange students!
