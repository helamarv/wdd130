São Paulo

São Paulo is a state in the southeast of Brazil. It is bordered by Rio de Janeiro to the east, Minas Gerais to the north and northeast, Mato Grosso do Sul to the west, Paraná to the south, and the Atlantic Ocean to the southeast. The state is an important center of business, education, technology, culture, and transportation, and is one of the largest economic regions in Latin America. São Paulo city, the state capital and largest city, is known for its diversity, vibrant nightlife, museums, restaurants, and strong financial influence.
